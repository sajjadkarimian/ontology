IPC Data Transparency Knowledge Graph

This landing page describes the dataset used in the Atlas study for modelling data transparency in Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) workflows.

About

This dataset supports the Atlas methodology for augmenting socio-technical systems analysis with machine-readable representations of data provenance, responsibility, and flow structure.
